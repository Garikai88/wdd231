About Me

I'am Garikai Munamato Mangobe, an author, educator, and digital entrepreneur from Zimbabwe. I am proudly based in Harare, the nation's capital. My journey blends creativity with technology. From publishing ebooks and music projects to designing software and building web solutions. Currently, I am studying Software Development at BYU Pathway as I have developed a speciality in web designing, branding, and responsive Development, with a growing expertise in Python and C#. I thrive on turning ideas into impactful digital products, whether it's fintech innovations like MobiBank+, educational platforms, or resilience-focused content. Driven by a vision to scale solutions globally, I bring resilience, creativity, and technical precision to every project that I pursue.
